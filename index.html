History of Jeonju, South Korea

Jeonju, a city possessing one-thousand years of history Jeonju, a city where the old meets the new. It is a city with strong ties to kings and democratic movements. Some of the greatest achievements in history took place here in Jeonju. Come partake in traditional cultural activities and experience the historical heritage of Korea.

Jeonju Hanok Village

Explore Korea's largest traditional village, consisting of over 700 hanoks house. Get a glimpse of the rich cultural heritage of South Korea through its well-preserved hanok houses, which are traditional Korean houses with a unique architectural style.

Bibimbap Festival

Join us in the heart of Jeonju for a culinary celebration like no other! Immerse yourself in the rich culinary heritage of Jeonju as we honor the beloved bibimbap tradition. Indulge in the tantalizing flavors and vibrant colors of bibimbap, Korea's iconic dish perfected over generations.

Gangneung Jungang Market

Experience a traditional Korean market established in 1980. Explore the markets two main areas; a floor housing a traditional market and an underground fishery market. Come visit the plethora of restaurants and coffee shops. As well as getting the opportunity to learn about the life of people in Gangneung.
